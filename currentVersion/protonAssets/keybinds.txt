###Client###
u - left click
y - right click
z - move forward
x - move backwards
c - move left
v - move right
b - jump
n - sprint
, - crouch
j - anti afk
f - disable all
m - credits
p - close client
l - help
h - client info
o - chat 1
i - chat 2
u - chat 3
/ - chat 4
[ - chat 5
] - chat 6
###Game###
w - forward
s - backward
a - left
d - right
space - jump
ctrl - sprint
shift - crouch
t - chat
q - drop item
###Other### (these are not keybinds)
n - gui state (m - set at program launch, y - yes, n - no)
y - enable logging (y - yes, n - no)
